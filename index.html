Services

Every last Tuesday of every month, we meet at Bing Wong Wash Center (2601 Telegraph Ave, Berkeley, California 94704) from 6-8pm.

Our volunteers provide free laundry services to those in need. We provide Quarters, Detergent, and Dryer sheets

About

Our Mission

  • Who We Are

    We are a God-loving and God-serving group of UC Berkeley students gathering across campus ministries and churches to love those in need through the form of free laundry. We have volunteers ranging from freshman to seniors, both young men and women, EECS majors to Political Science majors, introverted and extroverted, all eager to make tangible their love.

  • Join Us

    Every last Tuesday of every month, we host an event in which we provide quarters, detergent, and dryer sheets. In the time spent waiting for laundry to finish, we have conversations and build relationships with the homeless.
